# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Demonstrating empathy and kindness toward other people
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Accepting responsibility and apologizing to those affected by our mistakes
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- **Reporting reflection failures and false triggers honestly** — this is the highest-leverage contribution

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- The use of sexualized language or imagery
- Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Project-specific norms

reflect's domain (AI metacognition over user code) involves discussing failures candidly. Critique of the **prompt or implementation** is welcomed and encouraged. Personal critique of contributors is not.

When discussing reflection quality issues:
- Critique the **reflection output**, not the user who triggered it
- Anonymize tool call data unless contributor explicitly opts in
- Acknowledge that "reflect was wrong here" is a normal, useful finding

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the maintainer at **cj@chanjoongx.com**. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
